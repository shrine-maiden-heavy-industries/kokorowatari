# Kokorowatari

Kokorowatari is a [Torii] SDR/DDR SDRAM controller library. It's designed to be as feature-complete, fast, and size-efficient as possible.

For more information, see the [Introduction], and check out the [Getting Started] guide for how to quickly get up and running with Kokorowatari.

## Community

The two primary community spots for Kokorowatari are the `#torii` IRC channel on [libera.chat] (`irc.libera.chat:6697`) which you can join via your favorite IRC client or the [web chat], and the [discussion forum] on GitHub.

Please do join and share your projects, ask questions, get help with problems, or discuss Torii's development.

We also maintain a list of [projects using Kokorowatari], so post it in the [show and tell] section of the [discussion forum] and we'll add it!
